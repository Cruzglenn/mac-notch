What should we build next?

Here are 3 directions we could explore for Atoll to expand its utility as a notch assistant.

1

Window & Workspace Manager

Drag a window to the notch to access quick snap layouts (left/right/fullscreen) or send it to another workspace. Makes the notch a physical interaction point for macOS window management.

2

Quick AI Chat & Scratchpad

A fast, keyboard-driven pop-over from the notch for quick questions, text transformation (grammar, translation), or just jotting down a quick note without opening a full app.

3

Advanced Pomodoro & Focus

Deep integration with macOS Focus filters and Calendar. Instead of just a timer, it actively blocks distracting apps and manages work/break cycles, showing progress in the notch Live Activity.
